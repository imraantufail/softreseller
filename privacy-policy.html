Privacy Policy

Introduction

NumDesk is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website and use our services. By using our website, you agree to the terms of this privacy policy.

Collection of Personal Information

NumDesk collects personal information when you fill out forms on our website or contact us via email or phone. This information may include your name, email address, phone number, and other information that you provide us voluntarily. We may also collect information about your IP address and other technical information about your device and browser when you visit our website.

Use of Personal Information

NumDesk uses your personal information to contact you and provide you with the services you have requested. We may use your personal information to send you marketing communications about our products and services, but only if you have given us your consent to do so.

Disclosure of Personal Information

NumDesk does not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your personal information with third-party service providers who help us deliver our services or who provide marketing or other services on our behalf. We require these service providers to protect your personal information and use it only for the purposes for which we have engaged them.

We may also disclose your personal information if required to do so by law or if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to (a) comply with legal process or other legal requirements of any governmental authority; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of NumDesk; (c) enforce this privacy policy; (d) protect the interests of our users or others; or (e) operate or conduct maintenance and repair of our services or equipment, as authorized by law.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

NumDesk uses cookies and similar technologies to collect information about your use of our website. This information may include your IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited, and other information. We use this information to analyze trends, administer our website, track user movements around our website, and gather demographic information about our user base as a whole.

Advertising

NumDesk may use third-party advertising partners to display ads on our website. These advertising partners may use cookies and similar technologies to collect information about your use of our website and other websites in order to serve you targeted ads based on your interests. NumDesk does not provide personal information to our advertising partners, but they may collect information about your device and browser in order to serve you targeted ads.

Security

NumDesk takes reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no data transmission over the internet or storage system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee the security of your personal information.

Your Rights

You have the right to access and correct your personal information that we have collected. You also have the right to request that we delete your personal information, subject to our legal obligations to retain certain information. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us using the contact information provided below.
